Franklin Breaks Record

Franklin -- The Franklin West girls basketball team entered the Open Division State Championship with a 33-2 record. One of those losses came to their opponent in Saturday's state final, the Greenville Bobcats. On Saturday night in Franklin, the Golden Eagles won the game that mattered most, a 44-40 victory in the State Championship for the 3rd year in a row, seeting a new state record. In a game of runs and momentum, Franklin went on a 12-0 run to end the 1st quarter to take a 15-12 lead. Greenville battled back and led 27-24 at the break. Franklin struggled for much of the 2nd half and trailed 37-30 in the 4th quarter. Senior guard Robbie Jo Evans took control, scoring 11 straight points which were part of a 14-5 Franklin run that sealed the 44-40 victory.
