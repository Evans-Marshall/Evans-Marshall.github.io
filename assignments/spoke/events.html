Cycle the Divide!

  • Sponsored by the Spoke, this is the premier biking event of the season.
  • A 1, 2, or 3 day trek straddling the continental divide between Idaho and Montana.
  • For all skill levels and abilities. You choose the length of your ride.
  • Bring your own camping and riding gear, or let us outfit you for the ride.
  • Personal guides available starting at $150 per day.
  • Ride casually, or enter the tournament for rewards and prizes.
